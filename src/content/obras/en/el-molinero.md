This three-act zarzuela, with a libretto by Luis de Eguílaz, is considered one of the pinnacles of the "género grande". Premiered at the Teatro de la Zarzuela on December 21, 1870, it tells a story of honor and loyalty set in 12th-century Navarre.

The music, vibrant and full of local color, includes unforgettable numbers such as the millers' chorus and Gonzalo's romanza. This critical edition recovers the original orchestration, removing later additions.
